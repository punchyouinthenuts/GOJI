Below are instructions on how to begin a Weekly RAC job in the GOJI application. This window will update as you progress through the job.

  1. Navigate to the JOB DATA section
    1. Enter the job numbers into the appropriate fields
    2. Select the current year, month and week for the job
  2. Click DATA LOCK; this will create the job in the program, and lock the data in so it can't be altered mid-job
    1. If any data was entered incorrectly then click JOB DATA EDIT
    2. Correct all erroneous data
    3. Click DATA LOCK
